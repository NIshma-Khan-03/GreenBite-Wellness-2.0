‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is GreenBite all about? +

GreenBite is your one-stop wellness platform! We help you maintain a healthy lifestyle through nutritious recipes, fitness guidance, calorie tracking, and mindfulness practices. Our goal is to make healthy living simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone.

How accurate is the calorie calculator? +

Our calorie calculator uses scientifically proven formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor equation) to estimate your BMR and TDEE. While it provides a good starting point, individual needs may vary based on genetics, health conditions, and other factors. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

Are the recipes suitable for special diets? +

Our recipes focus on wholesome, nutritious ingredients and can be adapted for various dietary needs. Many are naturally vegetarian-friendly, and we provide ingredient lists so you can easily modify recipes to suit allergies, intolerances, or specific diet plans like vegan, gluten-free, or keto.

Can I use the workout generator if I'm a beginner? +

Absolutely! Our workout generator includes options for all fitness levels. Choose bodyweight exercises if you're just starting out, and always listen to your body. Start with shorter sessions and gradually increase intensity. If you have any health concerns, please consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new exercise routine.

Is my personal information safe on GreenBite? +

Your privacy is important to us! All personal data you enter (like your measurements for the calorie calculator) is stored locally on your device and never sent to external servers. We only collect basic information like newsletter subscriptions, which we handle securely and never share with third parties.
